FAQ

Questions parents ask first.

Is Hush safe for my child?

Every prompt is filtered. Every theme is age-appropriate. Stories run through a parent-built guidelines library before reaching your screen.

How does the AI work?

Hush uses a fine-tuned story model and a separate illustration model trained on classic children's book aesthetics. You give us the spark — name, age, interests — and we write a real story around your child.

What ages work best?

Ages 2–9. Story length, vocabulary, and themes all scale with the age you set. A 2-year-old gets short, rhyming sentences. A 9-year-old gets chapters.

Can I edit the story after it's created?

Yes. Tap any sentence to rewrite it. Swap illustrations. Or regenerate the whole thing — no extra charge.

Is my child's data private?

Always. We never sell data. We never train on your stories. Delete your account and every story in one click.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no fees, no dark patterns. Your library stays yours forever.
