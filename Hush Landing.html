Frequently asked

The questions parents ask first.

Is Hush safe for my child?

Every prompt is filtered, every theme age-appropriate. Stories are reviewed against a parent-built guidelines library before reaching your screen.

How does the AI actually work?

Hush uses a fine-tuned story model and an illustration model trained on classic children's book aesthetics. You give the spark, we write the book.

What ages is Hush for?

Kids ages 2 through 9. The story length, vocabulary, and themes scale with the age you set.

Can I edit a story after it's generated?

Yes. Tap any line to rewrite it, swap illustrations, or regenerate the whole story.

Is my child's data private?

Always. We never sell data, never train on your stories, and you can delete everything in one click.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no contracts, no questions. Your library stays yours.
